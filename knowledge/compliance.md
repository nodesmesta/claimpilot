# Compliance Requirements

## Regulatory Framework
- **NAIC Model Laws** (National Association of Insurance Commissioners)
- **State-specific regulations**
- **Fair Claims Settlement Practices**

## Timelines
- **Acknowledgement**: Within 15 days of claim receipt
- **Decision**: Within 30 days of complete documentation
- **Payment**: Within 5 business days of approval
- **Appeal**: 30-day window after decision

## Documentation Requirements
### Mandatory Records
1. **Claim file** with all communications
2. **Investigation notes** and rationale
3. **Evidence review** logs
4. **Decision documentation** with reasoning
5. **Compliance checklist**

### Audit Trail
- All communications timestamped
- Agent decisions with supporting facts
- Escalation paths documented
- Fraud indicators tracked
- Final decision justification

## Privacy & Data Protection
- **HIPAA compliance** for medical claims
- **PCI DSS** for payment information
- **Customer consent** for investigation
- **Data retention**: 7 years minimum

## Reporting Requirements
- **Fraud reports** to NICB (National Insurance Crime Bureau)
- **Regulatory filings** annually
- **Consumer complaint** tracking
- **Suspicious Activity Reports** (SARs)

## Agent Conduct
- **Impartial investigation**
- **No conflict of interest**
- **Full transparency** with policyholders
- **Document all decisions**
- **Escalate appropriately**

## Penalties for Non-Compliance
- Fines up to $10,000 per violation
- License suspension/revocation
- Civil lawsuits
- Reputational damage
