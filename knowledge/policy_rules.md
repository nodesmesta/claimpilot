# Insurance Policy Rules & Exclusions

## Auto Insurance Coverage

### Standard Coverage
- **Collision**: Damage from impact with another vehicle/object
- **Comprehensive**: Non-collision damage (theft, fire, vandalism)
- **Liability**: Damage/injury to others
- **Medical Payments**: Medical expenses for policyholder/passengers

### Exclusions
- **Intentional damage**
- **Racing or organized competition**
- **Using vehicle for commercial purposes** without endorsement
- **Under the influence** of drugs/alcohol
- **Unlicensed drivers**

### Deductibles
- Standard: $500
- Options: $250, $500, $1000, $2500
- Applies per claim

### Coverage Limits
- **Actual Cash Value (ACV)**: Market value at time of loss
- **Repair vs Total Loss**: Vehicle totaled if repair > 70% of ACV
- **Diminished Value**: Not covered unless specific endorsement

### Claim Requirements
1. Incident report within 24 hours for hit-and-run
2. Police report if damage > $1,000 or injury involved
3. Photo evidence required
4. Repair estimate from certified shop
5. Witness statements (if available)

### Payout Calculation
```
Settlement = min(ACV, Repair Cost) - Deductible
Partial approval for non-covered items
```

### Special Cases
- **Hit-and-run**: Coverage if filed within 7 days
- **Uninsured motorist**: Requires police report
- **Rental car**: Optional coverage, $50/day limit
