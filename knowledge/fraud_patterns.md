# Fraud Patterns in Insurance Claims

## Vehicle Insurance Fraud

### Common Patterns
1. **Staged Accidents**
   - Hit-and-run with no witnesses
   - High damage disproportionate to incident description
   - Multiple parties colluding to fake accidents

2. **Inflated Damages**
   - Claim amount >3x typical repair cost for vehicle
   - Adding pre-existing damage to new claim
   - Exaggerating repair needs

3. **Suspicious Timing**
   - Recent policy increase before claim
   - Multiple claims within short timeframe (>2 in 12 months)
   - Claims filed just before policy expiration

4. **Documentation Issues**
   - Inconsistent dates between incident and reporting
   - Photos with inconsistent lighting/background
   - Missing required documents

### Red Flags
- Amount >$15,000
- 0 witnesses + no police report
- Prior claims >2 in 12 months
- Filing >7 days after incident
- Vehicle value < claim amount

### Investigation Checklist
- Verify repair shop certification
- Check witness statements consistency
- Cross-reference vehicle history reports
- Verify police report authenticity
- Analyze photo metadata
